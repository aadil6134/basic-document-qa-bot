BookExpert Sample Company Handbook

Vacation Policy
Full-time employees receive 18 paid vacation days per calendar year. Unused vacation days do not roll over unless a written exception is approved by HR before December 15.

Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week. Team leads can approve fully remote arrangements for roles that do not require in-office equipment.

Expense Policy
Business travel expenses must be submitted within 30 days of the trip ending. Receipts are required for any single expense above 25 USD.

Support Hours
Customer support operates Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM local office time. Emergency production incidents are handled by the on-call rotation.
